FAQ's

How can I trust you with my things? Documents, etc?

We believe people should be able to trust apps freely. Our special delivery box is a symbol of that. You can send, deliver, fetch anything, totally relaxed knowing that your items are safe, and cannot be tampered or messed with. How cool is that?

What if the ‘friend’ runs away with my package?

We have an insurance option! If you believe your item is valuable, then simply opt for ‘insure my package’ in the app, you can insure an amount of until INR 25’000/-

In any unfortunate event, this amount will be deducted from the delivery friend’s account, and will be transferred to your bank within 3-7 working days.

How are you any different from all the other apps?

Your friends (Our delivery agents) will lock your package in the special delivery box.

Once the box is closed, it cannot be opened, tampered or messed with.

You will soon receive a 4 digit code. This is your secret pin to open the box. If you’re fetching something? Enter the code upon arrival. Sending a package to someone else? Share the secret pin to the person you’re sending the package to.
